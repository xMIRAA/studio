# **App Name**: Mirsadh's Horizon

## Core Features:

- Professional Hero Section: Prominently display Mohomed Mirsadh's full name, tagline, professional profile photo, and call-to-action buttons for 'View Portfolio' and 'Contact Me'.
- Detailed About Me & Education: An expanded personal bio section covering academic background, career aspirations in Business Analysis, Management IT, or Data Analytics, and core professional mindset.
- Categorized Skills Showcase: Display a comprehensive list of skills organized into categories like Data & Business Tools, Programming & Web Technologies, and Creative & Emerging Skills.
- Interactive Project Portfolio: Present projects (e.g., Hotel Reservation Website, Vehicle Service Station Website) with descriptions, tech stack, and placeholders for screenshots to highlight practical experience.
- Service Offerings Display: A 'What I Can Help With' section detailing current service capabilities such as Website Development, Content Writing, and Data Analysis, indicating ongoing development alongside academic growth.
- Simple Business AI Consultation Tool: A basic interactive tool where visitors can input a simple business problem and receive an AI-driven solution concept, demonstrating Mohomed's skills in AI-driven solutions for business needs.
- Integrated Contact Form: A user-friendly contact form with fields for name, email, and message, alongside clearly displayed contact information like email, phone, and LinkedIn profile URL.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme is light, reflecting professionalism and a modern aesthetic.
- Primary color: A warm, professional blue (#3388CC) conveying analytical strength and approachability.
- Background color: A subtle, desaturated light blue (#ECF0F3) providing a clean and spacious canvas.
- Accent color: A crisp cyan-teal (#32CDCD) for highlighting interactive elements and call-to-actions, aligning with forward-looking and creative aspects.
- Headings will use the sans-serif font 'Space Grotesk' for a modern, tech-savvy and forward-looking feel.
- Body text will use the sans-serif font 'Inter' for clear readability, professionalism, and neutrality across all content.
- Use clean, professional line-based icons throughout the website to maintain an uncluttered and modern appearance.
- Implement a spacious, grid-based layout that is uncluttered and fully mobile-responsive across all screen sizes.
- Incorporate subtle hover effects and soft transitions to enhance user interaction and provide a polished user experience without being distracting.
